Where are my royalties?

Congratulations, your song just made it onto Spotify. How exciting! With all this new found fame you’re probably already picturing yourself doing all kinds of cool stuff with all your hard earned money. But how does getting your money work? When are your royalties getting paid and how much is it going to be?

01

Your song

That’s right, we won’t start from the beginning, where you actually wrote the song or recorded it. We will start with the finished song on Spotify, because we will follow the flow of the money back to you. Along the way we will come across all the different parties who worked hard to make this all happen.

02

Digital radio

The digital radio is radio via the internet and is divided between the Interactive radio and the Non-interactive radio. Non-interactive radio works the same as Terrestrial radio. It won’t let the user choose what songs to listen to and in what order. Interactive radio lets the listener choose what songs to listen to and in what order, for example Spotify.
